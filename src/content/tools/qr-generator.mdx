## How to use this tool

1. Enter your text or URL
2. Customize size and colors
3. Choose error correction level
4. Download as PNG or SVG

## Options

**Size** — Choose from 128px to 1024px.

**Error Correction** — Higher levels allow more damage tolerance but create denser codes:

- Low (~7% recovery)
- Medium (~15% recovery)
- Quartile (~25% recovery)
- High (~30% recovery)

**Colors** — Customize foreground and background colors.

## Output formats

**PNG** — Raster image, good for most uses.

**SVG** — Vector image, perfect for print or scaling.

**Data URI** — Copy the Base64 data URL for embedding in HTML/CSS.

## Tips

- Use shorter URLs for cleaner QR codes
- Use URL shorteners for long links
- Test QR codes before printing
- Ensure sufficient contrast between colors

## Privacy

All QR codes are generated in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
